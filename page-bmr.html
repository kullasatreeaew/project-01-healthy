Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)

            Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the rate of the body's daily metabolic needs. or the minimum number of calories needed in daily life Therefore, the BMR calculation will help you calculate your daily calorie intake to maintain your current weight. And as we get older, it becomes more difficult to control our weight because our BMR decreases. Dieting is one of the reasons for the decrease in BMR. "Exercise regularly" to increase the efficiency of the metabolism, which will prevent the BMR from dropping too quickly.
