Body Mass Index (BMI)

            Body Mass Index (BMI) is the ratio of weight to height. used to indicate fat or thin In adults aged 20 years and over, the importance of knowing BMI to see the risk of various diseases If the computed value too much or too little because if you are obese There is a risk of high blood pressure, diabetes, ischemic heart disease. and gallstones in the gallbladder but at the same time who is too thin will be at risk of infection Decreased performance in the body Therefore, should maintain a normal weight level.
